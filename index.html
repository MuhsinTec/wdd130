Uganda – The Pearl of Africa

Uganda, known as the “Pearl of Africa,” is a beautiful country in East Africa. It is famous for its green landscapes, diverse wildlife, and rich cultural heritage. Uganda is home to Lake Victoria, one of the largest lakes in the world, and the Rwenzori Mountains. The country is known for its friendly people and natural beauty, making it an important destination for tourism and culture in Africa.
